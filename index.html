Introducing famlink

Stay connected with the people that matter the most

"Where are you?"

Don't waste time on texts and phone calls. Famlink lets you know where everyone is, without invading their privacy. This means no exact coordinates displayed on maps, but you'll know when someone is at a place that matters.

Peace of mind for your family
Mom doesn't have to blast your phone with texts and calls. Quickly know if dad's still at work or if your brother's at hockey practice. Famlink handles everything for you, giving your family the peace of mind they deserve.

Convenience for your roommates
Get rid of unproductive group chats filled with “Where are you?” messages. Famlink sends notifications relevant to everyone's current location, like if your roommate is at the grocery store and you need to let them know you're out of eggs!

At a glance,

See where everyone is

The Famlink Clock features a design that puts a spin on the traditional clock. Around the Clock are location icons, with people represented as a colored light pointing to where they're currently at. This piece of hardware makes a great addition to your living room shelf or kitchen counter. Don't go to the gym? Switch that icon with a different one to suit your family's needs.

On the go? No problemo!

Keep in touch with the app

Set up your addresses for each of the 12 locations and the app will take care of the rest, automatically updating your location status for you. Enable push notifications so you know when someone's at the grocery store, and send them a quick message in the group chat. (Note that you can use the app alone, but the Clock will need the app to work!)
