See where everyone is

The Famlink Clock features a design that puts a spin on the traditional clock. Instead of times, there are location icons. Colored lights represent a person, pointing to where they're currently at. This piece of hardware makes a great addition to your living room shelf or kitchen counter.

On the go? No problemo!

Keep in touch with the app

The Famlink App is what automatically updates your location status. You can also use it to check the Clock on the go. Headed to the grocery store? Enable push notifications so your fam automatically knows!

How it works

01.

Download the app.

On the app, for each of the 12 locations, you set up your own address.

02.

Enjoy automatic updates.

Famlink will automatically update your status when you visit any of the 12 locations. Enable notifications to get the useful location-based messages notifying you when someone's at the store.

03.

Connect it to the Clock.

Get the Clock and invite everyone in the family to join!
